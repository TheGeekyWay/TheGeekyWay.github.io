About Us

Posted on Sat 16 November 2013 by sanyam_khurana in misc

The Geeky Way is a technical blog founded in 2013, dedicated to making technology accessible and helping developers grow. We cover Linux, DevOps, Cloud, Web Development, Python, and more.

Founder

Sanyam Khurana

Sanyam is a Principal Engineer & Cloud Architect with over 10 years of experience building scalable, cloud-native backend systems. He holds an MSCS from Georgia Tech (specializing in Machine Learning) and is an AWS Solutions Architect and Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA). He goes by "CuriousLearner" across the web and is a maintainer at DjangoProject.com, a CPython bug triager, and notably implemented the helpful print() syntax error suggestion in Python 3. He has his name listed in the credits section of every browser ever released by Mozilla. You can find him on GitHub, X, and sanyamkhurana.com.
